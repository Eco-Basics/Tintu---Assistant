# Planning Prompts

Used for daily summary and end-of-day review generation.
Prompts are defined as constants in app/llm/prompts.py.

## Daily summary
Sections: Summary, Completed, Open tasks, Reminders set, Decisions, Open loops.

## End-of-day review
Sections: Completed today, Still open, What changed, Upcoming deadlines, Suggested first steps for tomorrow.
